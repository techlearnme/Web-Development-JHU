Three Form Of Matter

Solid

Have a fixed shape and cannot flow, because their particles cannot move from place to place and cannot be compressed (squashed), because their particles are close together and have no space to move into

Liquid

Flow and take the shape of their container, because their particles can move around each other and cannot be compressed, because their particles are close together and have no space to move into

Gas

Flow and completely fill their container, because their particles can move quickly in all directions and can be compressed, because their particles are far apart and have space to move into
